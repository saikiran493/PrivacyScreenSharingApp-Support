Privacy Policy

Privacy Screen Sharing Tool is built on a simple principle: what happens on your Mac stays on your Mac. This policy explains exactly what we do — and don't — collect.

Overview

Privacy Screen Sharing Tool ("we", "our", "the App") respects your privacy completely. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Nothing leaves your Mac.

Zero data collected or transmitted

Data collection

  • Screen recording: The app only accesses your screen when you explicitly start the screen sharing feature. You must grant macOS permission for screen recording. No screen content is ever transmitted outside your system.
  • Feedback: Feedback is collected only when you voluntarily share it via email or an App Store review. The app has no telemetry, analytics, or background data collection of any kind.

Data use

We do not collect any form of data. There is nothing to use, share, or sell. The app operates entirely on your device.

Data storage

Screen content is never stored locally or transmitted to any server. All screen data exists temporarily in memory purely for display purposes and is discarded the moment the app session ends.
