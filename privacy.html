Privacy Policy

Privacy Screen Sharing ("we", "our", "the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data Collection

  • Device Identifier: We store a unique device ID in your Keychain to enforce the 7-day trial. This ID is not linked to any personal information.
  • Feedback: If you provide feedback, it is sent to our Firebase Firestore database. No personal information is collected unless you include it in your feedback.
  • Screen Recording: The app only records your screen when you explicitly start the screen sharing feature. You must grant macOS permission for screen recording.

Data Use

We use the data solely to provide app functionality, enforce trial limits, and receive feedback to improve the app. We do not share your data with third parties.

Data Storage

Screen content is never stored locally or transmitted. All recordings exist temporarily in memory for display purposes and are discarded when the app is stopped.

Popovers / Feedback

The first popover appears after 24 hours and collects minimal feedback via button selection only. The second popover appears after the 7-day trial, allowing optional email input for new version notifications. No other data is collected or shared.
