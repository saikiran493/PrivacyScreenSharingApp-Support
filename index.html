Features
Everything you need to
present with confidence

Selective window hiding

Choose exactly which apps and windows to hide. Messages, WhatsApp, banking apps — gone from viewer's screen, still visible to you.

Notification masking

Banner alerts and Notification Center stay hidden from your audience at all times — even mid-presentation.

Cursor overlay

New in v2.4 — visualize your cursor during presentations, or optionally hide the real cursor from screen capture.

Auto pause

Mirroring automatically pauses the moment you interact with a hidden window — no accidental reveals, ever.

Floating toolbar

A compact bar gives you instant access to pause, resume, stop, and window controls — without leaving your current app.

Rock-solid stability

Zero crash rate and smooth, reliable performance across all sessions. It just works, every time.
